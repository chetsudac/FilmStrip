Near the end of the film, Carl comes across an addition to Ellie's Adventure Book. Unbeknownst to her husband, Ellie had added photos of their happy life together in the Adventure Book and a final message saying:
" Thanks for the adventure - now go have a new one! "
Her heartwarming note reinvigorates Carl at last, and he moves on from losing Ellie to living his life to the fullest.
